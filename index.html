Course Orientation

We treat diet as a daily ritual of alignment: digestion is transformation, timing is law, and restraint is power. Each week includes teachings + practice + a closing insight.

Operating Rules (all 8 weeks):
  • Eat with intention; stop before full.
  • Prioritize warm, simple meals.
  • Remove snacking; protect digestion.
  • Hydrate with warm water/tea unless medically contraindicated.
